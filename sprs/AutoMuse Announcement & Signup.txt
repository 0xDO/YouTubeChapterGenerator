- David Shapiro announces Auto Muse, an AI toolset for fiction writers.
- Auto Muse aims to assist and educate aspiring writers.
- Shapiro has experience with GPT-3 and AI in creative writing.
- Auto Muse includes a synopsis generator and plot generator.
- Future plans involve scene generation and full story development.
- Shapiro acknowledges concerns about AI impacting writers and editors.
- Auto Muse targets aspiring writers, not professionals.
- Closed beta limited to 15 people, prioritizing Patreon supporters.
- Auto Muse seeks user feedback and stress testing.
- Interested users can sign up at automews.io or support on Patreon.
